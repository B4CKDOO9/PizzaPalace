About Us

Our Story 🦈

Shark's Pizza was born from a wild idea: what if a shark could eat pizza? The answer was — of course, and EVERY DAY.

Founded in 2019 by the sea, our pizzeria combines the freshness of the Adriatic coast with the Italian tradition of dough making. Every morning our masters mix the dough by hand, and we cook the sauce using a recipe we guard like a sailor's secret.

Today we deliver across the entire city and proudly serve more than 10,000 happy guests who always come back for one more bite.
